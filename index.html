Hello World

I am Puneet Mathur, an MS CS grad student at University of Maryland, College Park. I did my bachelors in computer engineering at NSIT, University of Delhi.
I have experience in Machine Learning, Computer Vision, NLP and Social Network Analysis. Previously, I was a research assistant at MIDAS Labs since January 2018. Currently, I am working on deep learning projects in NLP, multimedia retrieval, social media analysis, biomedical imaging, computer vision, bioinformatics and machine learning applications. Do check out my Youtube channel - theMADAIguy where I share my learnings with the world.
